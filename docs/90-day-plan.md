## Campaigns: What We're Keeping, What We're Killing

| Campaign | Decision | Rationale |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Birthday Bonus | Keep (fix automation first) | Works for existing loyalists |
| Refer a Friend | Rebuild | New hook: share your quiz result |
| Seasonal Drinks | Rebuild | Personalized by personality type |
| Happy Hour | Rebuild | Smart notifications, not blasts |
| App Download Bonus | Pause | Nothing to return to yet — relaunch after quiz is live |
| Double Points Weekend | Deprioritize | Discount treadmill. Only use sparingly if needed for a spike. |
| Early Bird | Kill | Rewards locked-in behavior. No incremental value. |
| Social Share Bonus | Kill | Paying for shares creates resentment |
| Survey Bonus | Kill | We know the problem. Stop re-diagnosing. |
| Visit Streak | Kill | Manufactured behavior, not real connection |

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| IT delays on quiz integration | Medium | Quiz prototype already built — integration is connection work, not design work |
| Low quiz completion rate | Low | Barista activation is the key driver — in-person prompt converts better than any push |
| CFO doesn't approve $28K | Low | Business case shows 3.2x conservative ROI. Prior $40K is sunk. Cost of cancellation is higher. |
| Barista adoption is inconsistent | Medium | Jennifer's coordination + Marcus Jr.'s peer credibility mitigates this |
| Personality distribution skews heavily to one type | Low | 6 questions designed to distribute across all 4 types. Monitor in week 1. |
