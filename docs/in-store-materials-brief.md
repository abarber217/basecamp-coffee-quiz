# In-Store Launch Materials Brief: Coffee Personality Quiz
**Project:** Basecamp Rewards — Quiz Launch
**For:** Design Team
**Requested by:** Loyalty Program Manager
**Date:** March 2026
**Materials needed by:** Week 3 (April 6, 2026)
**Print deadline:** Week 4 (April 13, 2026) — must be in all 45 locations before launch

---

## Overview

We're launching a Coffee Personality Quiz in the Basecamp Rewards app. Members answer 6 questions, get a coffee identity (Zen Minimalist, Sweet Enthusiast, etc.), and receive a personalized drink recommendation.

In-store materials drive quiz completions by catching people at the right moment — when they're sitting with their coffee and have 60 seconds to spare. The tone should feel like Basecamp: warm, a little playful, never corporate.

**Live quiz URL for QR codes:** https://quiz-project-xi-livid.vercel.app

---

## Materials Needed

### 1. Table Tent Card
**Quantity:** 3–4 per location × 45 locations = ~180 cards
**Size:** 4" × 6" folded tent (standard)
**Placement:** Every table and window seat
**Finish:** Matte, uncoated — matches Basecamp's tactile, artisan feel

**Purpose:** Catch people while they're sitting and relaxed. This is the primary quiz driver.

**Copy:**

> *Front panel:*
>
> **What kind of coffee person are you?**
>
> Bold espresso? Cold brew pragmatist? Matcha oat latte explorer?
>
> Find out in 60 seconds.
>
> [QR code]
> *Open your Basecamp Rewards app or scan to take the quiz*

> *Back panel:*
>
> **Four personalities. One of them is yours.**
>
> ☕ The Zen Minimalist
> 🍮 The Sweet Enthusiast
> 🍵 The Wellness Explorer
> ⚡ The Practical Pragmatist
>
> *Take the quiz and discover where you belong.*

**Design notes:**
- Warm cream background (#F5EFE6) — matches quiz app
- Lora serif for headlines, clean sans-serif for body
- QR code should be large enough to scan easily from a seated position
- Emojis for the four personalities should feel intentional, not clip-art
- No discount language anywhere — this is about identity, not a deal

---

### 2. Counter Card
**Quantity:** 2 per location × 45 locations = ~90 cards
**Size:** 4" × 5" standing card (A6 landscape)
**Placement:** Register area and drink pickup counter
**Finish:** Matte

**Purpose:** Quick hit during the transaction. Baristas can gesture to it when mentioning the quiz. Short copy — people at the counter aren't stopping to read.

**Copy:**

> **What's your coffee personality?**
>
> Take the 60-second quiz in the Basecamp Rewards app.
>
> [QR code]

**Design notes:**
- Even simpler than the table tent — headline, one line, QR code
- QR code dominates the card
- Same warm cream palette
- Can include the four emoji + personality names in small text at the bottom if space allows

---

### 3. Window Cling (Optional — Recommend for High-Traffic Locations)
**Quantity:** 1 per high-traffic location (~15 flagship stores)
**Size:** 8" × 10"
**Placement:** Front window or door, eye level
**Finish:** Static cling, removable

**Purpose:** Awareness for people passing by or entering. Creates curiosity before they even order.

**Copy:**

> **Find your coffee personality inside.**
>
> [QR code]
> *Take the quiz in the Basecamp Rewards app*

**Design notes:**
- Can be more visually bold — this is seen from a distance
- Consider a split design showing two of the four personality emojis/names to create intrigue
- White or cream background so it reads clearly against the window

## Brand Guidelines Reminder

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "community," "neighborhood," "baristas," "coffee ritual" | "customers," "our stores," "team members" |
| Warm and conversational | Corporate or stiff |
| Lora serif for headlines | All-caps enthusiasm |
| Cream/warm palette (#F5EFE6) | Bright promotional colors |
| Identity-focused copy | Discount or points language |

---

## QR Code Specs

- **Destination URL:** https://quiz-project-xi-livid.vercel.app
- **Format:** SVG preferred, PNG at minimum 300dpi
- **Minimum print size:** 1" × 1" (test scan before print)
- **Error correction:** Level H (highest) — accounts for café environment wear
- **Color:** Dark on light background only (no inverted/white QR codes)
